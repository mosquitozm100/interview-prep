# Behaviour Question Bank

## Tell Me About Yourself

- Walk me through your background.
- What kind of engineer are you?
- What are you looking for next?

## Why This Role?

- Why backend / AI backend?
- Why evals or agent workflow infrastructure?
- What parts of this role match your experience?

## Why This Company?

- Why this company now?
- What product or engineering problems interest you?

## Conflict

- Tell me about a disagreement with a teammate or stakeholder.
- Tell me about a time you changed someone's mind.

## Ambiguity

- Tell me about a project with unclear requirements.
- How do you make progress when the goal is not well defined?

## Failure

- Tell me about a mistake or failed project.
- What did you learn and change afterward?

## Leadership / Ownership

- Tell me about a time you owned a project end to end.
- Tell me about a time you improved team velocity or quality.

## Technical Deep Dive

- Explain a technically difficult project.
- What tradeoffs did you make?
- How did you validate the system?

## Project Impact

- What was the impact of your work?
- How did you measure success?
- What would you do differently?

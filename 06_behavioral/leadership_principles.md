# Behavioral Themes

## Ownership

Show that you took responsibility beyond the narrow task, clarified priorities, and followed through.

## Ambiguity

Show how you created structure when requirements, ownership, or success metrics were unclear.

## Conflict

Show calm disagreement, evidence-based reasoning, and alignment toward the shared goal.

## Impact

Use verified outcomes only. If metrics are uncertain, say `TODO` until confirmed.

## Technical Depth

Explain the design choice, alternatives, constraints, and why your approach was appropriate.

## Collaboration

Name stakeholders by role, not private identity. Explain how you aligned and communicated.

## Learning From Failure

Own the miss, describe the correction, and show what changed afterward.
